Federal Decree-Law No. 32 of 2021 on Commercial Companies (excerpt)

Article 5 – Ownership
1. A company may be established by one or more natural or legal persons.
2. Foreign ownership in certain activities may reach up to 100% as per the relevant Cabinet decisions.
3. The minimum share capital for a limited liability company shall be as prescribed by the relevant authority.

Article 10 – Management
1. The company shall be managed by one or more managers appointed by the shareholders.
2. Terms of appointment and removal shall be set out in the company’s Articles of Association.
