AAOIFI Shari’ah Standard on Murabaha (SSM-1) – Key Principles

1. Murabaha is a sale contract where the seller discloses its cost and profit margin to the buyer.
2. The asset must exist and be owned by the seller before the commencement of the contract.
3. Payment may be deferred or in installments, as agreed in the contract.
4. No extra charges are allowed except those expressly agreed upon.
