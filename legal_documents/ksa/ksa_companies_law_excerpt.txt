Royal Decree No. M/3 (2015) – Companies Law (excerpt)

Article 8 – Founding Shareholders
1. A joint stock company shall be founded by not less than five natural or legal persons.
2. The founders must subscribe fully to the nominal value of their shares in cash or in kind.

Article 35 – Board of Directors
1. The board of directors shall consist of a minimum of three and a maximum of eleven members.
2. Directors shall be elected by the general assembly for a term not exceeding three years.
