LOAN AGREEMENT

THIS LOAN AGREEMENT (the "Agreement") is made and entered into on May 10, 2025, by and between:

LENDER: ABC Bank, a financial institution organized under the laws of UAE, with its principal place of business at Financial District, Dubai, UAE ("Lender")

AND

BORROWER: XYZ Company LLC, a limited liability company organized under the laws of UAE, with its principal place of business at Business Bay, Dubai, UAE ("Borrower")

WHEREAS, Borrower has requested Lender to extend a loan facility, and Lender has agreed to provide such facility subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

1. LOAN AMOUNT
   Lender agrees to lend to Borrower the sum of AED 1,000,000 (One Million UAE Dirhams) (the "Loan").

2. PURPOSE
   The Loan shall be used exclusively for business expansion purposes and not for any personal, family, or household purposes.

3. DISBURSEMENT
   The Loan shall be disbursed in a single tranche directly to Borrower's account within 5 business days of the execution of this Agreement.

4. TERM
   The term of the Loan shall be 24 months from the date of disbursement.

5. INTEREST RATE
   Borrower shall pay interest on the outstanding principal balance of the Loan at a fixed rate of 5% per annum, calculated on a 365-day year basis.

6. REPAYMENT
   6.1 Borrower shall repay the Loan in 24 equal monthly installments of AED 43,871, including principal and interest.
   6.2 The first installment shall be due and payable one month after the date of disbursement, with subsequent installments due on the same day of each month thereafter.
   6.3 All payments shall be made in UAE Dirhams to the account designated by Lender.

7. PREPAYMENT
   Borrower may prepay the Loan in whole or in part at any time without penalty. Any partial prepayment shall be applied first to accrued interest and then to principal.

8. LATE PAYMENT
   If any payment is not received within 10 days after its due date, Borrower shall pay a late fee equal to 5% of the overdue amount plus interest at the rate of 1% per month on the outstanding balance until paid in full.

9. SECURITY
   9.1 As security for the Loan, Borrower hereby grants to Lender a first-priority security interest in all equipment purchased using the Loan proceeds.
   9.2 Borrower shall execute all documents necessary to perfect Lender's security interest.

10. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES
    Borrower represents and warrants that:
    10.1 It is duly organized and validly existing under the laws of UAE.
    10.2 It has the power and authority to enter into this Agreement.
    10.3 The execution and delivery of this Agreement do not violate any agreement to which Borrower is a party.
    10.4 All financial information provided to Lender is true and correct in all material respects.

11. EVENTS OF DEFAULT
    Each of the following shall constitute an Event of Default:
    11.1 Failure to make any payment when due.
    11.2 Breach of any representation, warranty, or covenant.
    11.3 Insolvency or bankruptcy of Borrower.
    11.4 Any material adverse change in Borrower's financial condition.

12. REMEDIES
    Upon the occurrence of an Event of Default, Lender may:
    12.1 Declare the entire unpaid principal balance, together with accrued interest, immediately due and payable.
    12.2 Exercise all rights and remedies available under applicable law.

13. GOVERNING LAW
    This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the United Arab Emirates.

14. NOTICES
    All notices shall be in writing and delivered to the addresses set forth above, or to such other address as either party may designate by written notice.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the date first above written.
